# AI Clinical Decision Support & Patient Triage Assistant

## 1. Problem Statement
Healthcare providers face increasing patient loads, fragmented medical data, and limited time for clinical decision-making. This leads to delayed diagnosis, physician burnout, and inconsistent patient triage.

## 2. Objectives
- Improve patient triage efficiency
- Reduce time spent on medical documentation
- Provide structured, AI-generated clinical summaries
- Support healthcare professionals with evidence-based insights

## 3. Target Users
- Doctors and clinicians
- Nurses and triage staff
- Hospitals and clinics
- Telemedicine platforms

## 4. Key Features
- AI-based symptom intake chatbot
- Automated patient risk scoring
- Medical record summarization
- Suggested next steps and alerts
- Doctor-facing dashboard

## 5. Functional Requirements
- Accept patient symptom input (text/chat)
- Upload and parse medical reports (PDF/Text)
- Generate patient summary in structured format
- Classify patients into risk categories
- Display AI insights to clinicians
- Maintain patient history securely

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements
- High data privacy and security
- Low latency response time
- Scalable cloud-based architecture
- Explainable AI outputs
- Compliance with healthcare data regulations

## 7. Assumptions
- Final medical decisions are made by licensed professionals
- Patients provide accurate information
- AI models are continuously updated with medical guidelines

## 8. Constraints
- Limited availability of labeled medical datasets
- Regulatory and compliance challenges
